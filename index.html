Hello

UNCOMMON WOMAN,


We invite you to the Uncommon Woman Conference 2018. This year, our theme is INSPIRE. We promise you an amazing time in the presence of God and in the company of women like yourself who want to be inspired to birth God’s plans and purposes for their lives. You are in for a treat this year as we have a wonderful line up of inspiring women of God to bring words of life.
